# KDC_GLOSSARY.md

## Calibration
Process of preparing a facility for operations.

## Commissioning
Bringing a calibrated facility into revenue service.

## Compute Capacity
Raw computational output of installed hardware.

## Effective Compute Capacity
Compute capacity after modifiers and diminishing returns.

## Ownership Factor
Player-owned percentage of generated revenue.

## Distance Multiplier
Revenue bonus based on distance from Kerbin.

## Settlement Delay
Delay between revenue generation and revenue payout.

## EMA
Exponential Moving Average used for distance smoothing.

## Operational Health
Combined measure of uptime, thermal stability, power margin, and communications.

## Uptime
Percentage of time the facility remains operational.

## Thermal Efficiency
Measure of cooling effectiveness.

## Power Margin
Available electrical power beyond minimum requirements.

## Fault
Operational issue that reduces performance.

## MTBF
Mean Time Between Failures.

## Science Data
Research output generated by Science Mode.

## Operations Dashboard
Central management interface.

## CommNet Status
Connectivity state used by KDC systems.
