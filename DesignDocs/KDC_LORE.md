# KDC_LORE.md

## Background

As Kerbal civilization expanded beyond Kerbin, demand for computation increased dramatically.

Mission planning, scientific analysis, communications processing, and simulation workloads began exceeding the capacity of traditional ground-based systems.

Orbital data centers emerged as the solution.

These facilities transform power, cooling, communications infrastructure, and engineering expertise into profitable computational services.

## Themes

- Commercial space operations
- Orbital industry
- Long-term infrastructure
- Automation
- Reliability
- Engineering-driven profitability

## MechMort

The MechMort Computer Core is the flagship compute platform of KDC.

If MechJeb flies the rocket, MechMort flies the balance sheet.

Named in honor of Mortimer Kerman, MechMort embodies financial optimization through orbital infrastructure.

## Science Mode

Some advanced facilities operate in Science Mode.

Rather than directly generating Science, they produce Science Data that feeds into existing research systems.

This preserves exploration while rewarding investment in infrastructure.
