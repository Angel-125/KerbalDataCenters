# Kerbal Data Center (KDC)

## Overview

Kerbal Data Center (KDC) is a Career Mode-focused Kerbal Space Program mod that allows players to build orbital data centers and earn Funds through commercial computing operations.

Players launch compute infrastructure, provide power, thermal management, communications, calibration, and maintenance, then generate recurring revenue based on operational effectiveness.

Required Mods:
- Contract Configurator
- System Heat

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Launch compute infrastructure.
2. Establish stable orbital operations.
3. Calibrate and commission the facility.
4. Accept ownership and funding arrangements.
5. Generate compute revenue.
6. Maintain reliability and operational health.
7. Expand capacity.
8. Build a distributed interplanetary compute network.

## Design Pillars

- Orbital infrastructure should have a meaningful purpose.
- Revenue should reward engineering rather than passive waiting.
- Failures should reduce revenue rather than destroy vessels.
- Distance should matter.
- Infrastructure should scale naturally from early to late game.
- Micromanagement should be minimized.

## Key Systems

### Compute Modules
Generate compute capacity while consuming ElectricCharge and producing System Heat.

### Calibration & Commissioning
Required before revenue generation begins and whenever compute hardware changes.

### Revenue Generation
Revenue depends on effective compute capacity and operational health.

### Contracts & Ownership
Third parties may fund construction in exchange for a permanent revenue share.

### Distance Multipliers
Distance from Kerbin influences both revenue and payout timing.

### Faults & Maintenance
Faults reduce profitability until repaired.

### Operations Dashboard
Centralized interface for managing KDC infrastructure.

## Operational Health

Operational Health is influenced by:
- Uptime
- Thermal Stability
- Power Margin
- Communications Status
- Calibration State
